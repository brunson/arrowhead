Architectural Review Request Form


In an effort to maintain property values, the Declaration of Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions require that exterior changes be approved by the Architectural Review Committee prior to commencing work. This evaluation addresses architectural harmony, color, location, minimum construction standards and use restrictions.

To download a copy of the Architectural Review Request Form (ARR Form) please click HERE.

To download a copy of the Design Guidelines please click HERE.

Below are some tips that should hopefully make the ARR Form Request go smoother and reduce delays.

 

Paint Requests

When submitting a Paint Request you must specify the colors you are going to be painting the following 5 areas:

  • Body
  • Garage Door
  • Front Door
  • Trim
  • Accents

Replacing your Roof

Drip Edge/Flashing must be painted to match the exact same color of the existing house trim.
